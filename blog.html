Got Performance Metrics?
Using the annual review process to assess corporate culture.

How do you define performance metrics for a team of abstract impressionist painters? I don’t know either, but this is IT. Metrics abound. A company’s annual review form can reveal their top priorities, allowing you to decide whether your values align with their vision; however, beware the company who pays lip service to equity without creating policies to protect it. Performance reviews cannot be equitable without standardized performance metrics.

The RCD Unicorn
Wanted: RCD Unicorn. Preferred qualifications: Everything and the kitchen sink.

Universities everywhere have been earmarking funds for new staff positions to facilitate Research Computing and Data. By creating job postings with a laundry list of superhuman qualifications, we perpetuate a lack of diversity, increase the workload of existing staff, and set up our new hires for failure. Creating new staff positions at cash-strapped universities is often a once in a lifetime opportunity...let's make it count!
